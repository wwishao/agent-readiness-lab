## Tracking Rules

Lead stages:
- New
- Replied
- Wants checklist
- Wants paid kit
- Wants consulting
- Not now

Success signal before PayPal approval:
- 3+ people ask for the kit
- 1+ person asks about consulting
- 10+ clicks or comments from relevant audience

Do not:
- send bulk unsolicited email to scraped addresses
- post identical copy across many communities
- promise guaranteed ROI
- claim legal/security compliance certification
